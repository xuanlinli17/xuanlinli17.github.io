I am a second-year PhD student at UCSD CSE, advised by Prof. Hao Su (2021-). Previously I was an undergraduate majoring in Mathematics and Computer Science at UC Berkeley (2017-2021). GitHub  /  Google Scholar  /  LinkedIn  /  Twitter

Research

I am interested in many aspects of computer vision, robotics, and NLP. In particular, I'm interested in embodied AI and building general 3D/2D vision-language and policy models that are central to agent (e.g., robot) decision making, allowing agents to acquire generalizable skills (e.g., generalizable object manipulation) and accomplish generic tasks through data-driven, learning-based approaches.
